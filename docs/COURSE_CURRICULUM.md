# 📚 Course Curriculum - Maple Hospital Academy

Detailed curriculum outline for the complete Maple Hospital Medical Training Course.

---

## 📖 Overview

The Maple Hospital Academy curriculum is structured as a **progressive, competency-based learning path** with four units spanning from Beginner to Expert difficulty levels. Students advance through the course by completing lessons, assignments, and passing unit quizzes.

### Course Philosophy

- **Hands-On Learning:** Theory combined with practical in-game tasks
- **Progressive Difficulty:** Each unit builds on previous knowledge
- **Real-Time Feedback:** Immediate quiz scoring and progress tracking
- **Flexible Pacing:** Students progress at their own speed
- **Certification:** Graduation credential upon course completion

---

## 🎓 Course Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of Maple Hospital Academy, students will be able to:

✅ Navigate the hospital map and understand department locations
✅ Execute proper patient intake and diagnostic procedures
✅ Respond effectively to emergency codes (Code Blue, Code Gray)
✅ Administer medical treatments based on diagnoses
✅ Compound pharmaceuticals and manage pharmacy operations
✅ Coordinate with other medical staff during roleplay scenarios
✅ Track and manage patient progress using in-game systems
✅ Understand the mechanics of all major hospital roles

## 📋 Unit 1: Hospital Onboarding & Core Mechanics

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Duration:** 45-60 minutes

### Learning Objectives

- Understand the physical layout of Maple Hospital
- Identify key departments and their purposes
- Learn basic gameplay mechanics and controls
- Understand the experience (XP) system
- Begin your first patient interactions
- Master fast-leveling strategies

### Lessons

#### Lesson 1.1: Navigating Maple Hospital

**Content Overview:**
- Hospital layout and floor organization
- Key locations: Reception, ER, ICU, Pharmacy, Operating Rooms
- Elevator and stairwell navigation
- Department functions and staff roles

**Key Takeaways:**
- Reception & ER (Floor 1) - Patient entry point
- Maternity, PACU, Dentist (Floor 2) - Specialized care units
- ICU, Laboratory, Pharmacy (Floor 3) - Advanced care and compounding

**Time to Complete:** 15-20 minutes

#### Lesson 1.2: Basic Mechanics & Fast Leveling

**Content Overview:**
- XP earning mechanics and progression system
- Patient claiming and assignment flow
- Medical tools and equipment usage
- Bed management and patient flow
- Efficient leveling strategies
- Role unlock system (Level 5, 25, 50, 80, 100+)

**Key Takeaways:**
- Claim patients quickly to maximize XP
- Guide patients to beds for faster treatment completion
- NPC patients provide faster XP than players
- Each role has specific tools and interactions
- Leveling cap is infinite (no hard limit at level 100)

**Time to Complete:** 20-25 minutes

### Assignments

| Assignment | Description | In-Game Task | Estimated Time |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| 1.1 | **Full Hospital Tour** | Complete a lap around all three floors and locate key departments | 10 minutes |
| 1.2 | **First Patient** | Claim an NPC patient in the ER and guide them to an available bed | 5-10 minutes |
| 1.3 | **Reach Level 5** | Earn enough XP to unlock the Nurse role | 15-30 minutes |

## 📋 Unit 2: Diagnostics & Clinical Care

**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Duration:** 60-75 minutes

### Learning Objectives

- Master the diagnostic workflow
- Use scanning equipment correctly
- Understand patient symptoms and conditions
- Administer appropriate treatments
- Work with medical station equipment
- Coordinate patient flow through treatment pipeline

### Lessons

#### Lesson 2.1: The Diagnostic Flow

**Content Overview:**
- Patient symptom assessment
- Scanner/X-Ray machine operation
- Identifying common ailments: Broken Bones, Cardiac Issues, Viral Infections, Allergies
- Proper documentation on digital clipboard
- Preparation for treatment phase

**Key Takeaways:**
- Always diagnose before treating (unsafe roleplay to skip diagnosis)
- Bring patients to scanning rooms on Floor 1
- Record findings to plan correct treatment
- Different conditions require different treatment approaches
- Time spent on diagnosis leads to better treatment outcomes

**Time to Complete:** 20-25 minutes

#### Lesson 2.2: Administering Medical Treatments

**Content Overview:**
- Fracture and broken bone treatment (casts/bandages)
- Viral infection treatment (syringes, medications)
- Critical condition management (ICU placement)
- IV Saline bag attachment and monitoring
- Vital sign monitoring and patient recovery

**Key Takeaways:**
- Each condition has specific treatment requirements
- Move critical patients to ICU on Floor 3
- Monitor patient health bars throughout treatment
- Proper treatment leads to patient recovery and XP rewards
- Multiple staff members can collaborate on complex cases

**Time to Complete:** 25-30 minutes

### Assignments

| Assignment | Description | In-Game Task | Estimated Time |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| 2.1 | **Diagnostic Practice** | Diagnose an NPC patient using the X-Ray/Body Scanner machine | 10-15 minutes |
| 2.2 | **Fracture Treatment** | Successfully treat a patient diagnosed with a fracture by applying a cast | 10-15 minutes |
| 2.3 | **ICU Management** | Attach a Saline bag to an ICU patient on Floor 3 and monitor vitals | 15-20 minutes |

## 📋 Unit 3: Emergency Scenarios & Roleplay Codes

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Duration:** 60-75 minutes

### Learning Objectives

- Understand emergency response protocols
- Execute Code Blue procedures
- Handle Code Gray situations
- Coordinate team response to emergencies
- Use defibrillation and CPR equipment
- Maintain professional composure during crises

### Lessons

#### Lesson 3.1: Code Blue Protocols (Pretend Emergency)

**Content Overview:**
- Understanding Code Blue triggers and alerts
- Defibrillator (AED) location and usage
- CPR compression minigame mechanics
- Team coordination during resuscitation
- Post-resuscitation patient care
- Roleplay etiquette and communication

**Key Takeaways:**
- Code Blue = patient in cardiac or respiratory arrest (roleplay simulation)
- Immediate team response is critical
- Grab AED from wall mounts and apply pads
- Perform timed CPR compressions in sync with rhythm indicator
- Coordinate with other staff for medication preparation
- Maintain professional in-game roleplay atmosphere

**Time to Complete:** 20-25 minutes

#### Lesson 3.2: Code Gray Protocols (Pretend Safety)

**Content Overview:**
- Code Gray triggers: combative NPCs/players, disruptions
- De-escalation techniques in roleplay
- Security staff coordination
- Department lockdown procedures
- Restricted area access control
- Professional conduct during conflicts

**Key Takeaways:**
- Code Gray = disruptive individual (roleplay scenario)
- Attempt calm, professional communication first
- Call in-game Security if de-escalation fails
- Lock down sensitive areas (OR, Pharmacy) to prevent interference
- Maintain fun and realistic medical roleplay environment
- Document incident in in-game logs if available

**Time to Complete:** 20-25 minutes

### Assignments

| Assignment | Description | In-Game Task | Estimated Time |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| 3.1 | **Defibrillator Practice** | Grab a Defibrillator from the ER wall and practice the shock mechanic | 10-15 minutes |
| 3.2 | **Code Scenario** | Participate in a simulated Code Blue or Code Gray scenario with other players | 15-20 minutes |
| 3.3 | **Security Practice** | Successfully lock/unlock a security door as hospital staff member | 10-15 minutes |

## 📋 Unit 4: The Level 80 Pharmacist Mastery

**Difficulty:** Expert | **Duration:** 75-90 minutes

### Learning Objectives

- Master advanced pharmaceutical operations
- Understand chemical compound formulas
- Operate pharmacy management systems
- Prepare complex medication orders
- Manage inventory and prescription workflows
- Coordinate with medical staff on medication needs

### Lessons

#### Lesson 4.1: The Pharmacy Interface & Orders

**Content Overview:**
- Reaching Level 80 and unlocking Pharmacist role
- Order Terminal interface and prescription system
- Understanding doctor's orders
- Bottle selection and labeling station
- Order fulfillment workflow
- Priority management during high-volume periods

**Key Takeaways:**
- Pharmacist role unlocks at Level 80
- Most mechanically detailed role in Maple Hospital
- Check Order Terminal for active prescriptions
- Grab empty bottles from shelf
- Use digital labeler to print prescription labels
- Maintain accuracy to prevent order failures

**Time to Complete:** 25-30 minutes

#### Lesson 4.2: Compounding & Formula Synthesis

**Content Overview:**
- Reading prescription recipes
- Identifying colored liquid solutions
- Chemical compounding dispenser operation
- Precise volume measurement
- Pill sorting and counting
- Quality control checks
- Pre-mixing strategies for efficiency

**Key Takeaways:**
- Each prescription specifies exact chemical formula
- Example: Red Compound A + Blue Formula B
- Walk to appropriate dispensers and click to pour
- Mix components together in correct sequence
- Sort pills if order requires them
- Deliver to patient or leave in pick-up slot
- Pre-mix common formulas for instant order completion

**Time to Complete:** 30-35 minutes

### Assignments

| Assignment | Description | In-Game Task | Estimated Time |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| 4.1 | **Reach Level 80** | Level up your character to unlock the Pharmacist role (or assist level-80+ player) | 2-4 hours (or observe) |
| 4.2 | **Retrieve Prescription** | Access the Pharmacy Order Terminal and retrieve an active prescription order | 5-10 minutes |
| 4.3 | **Complete Order** | Successfully label an empty bottle, fill it with correct formula, and submit | 15-20 minutes |
